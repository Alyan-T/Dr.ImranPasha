FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I apply for PhD supervision under Prof. Pasha?
    Prospective PhD students should contact the National Institute of Food Science & Technology (NIFSAT) at UAF directly for admissions, and send a research statement and CV to ipasha2001@uaf.edu.pk.
  • Is Prof. Pasha available for international research collaborations?
    Yes. Prof. Pasha actively pursues international collaborative research. Please use the contact form above with "Research Collaboration" selected, and briefly describe your proposed project and institution.
  • How can I request a copy of a specific publication?
    Send an email with the title and journal reference to ipasha2001@uaf.edu.pk. Author copies of publications are shared subject to publisher copyright agreements.
  • Where can I find Prof. Pasha's ORCID or Web of Science profile?
    ORCID: 0000-0001-7106-8860. Web of Science Researcher ID: AAA-5186-2019. Both are also listed in the contact details above.
  • Can Prof. Pasha be invited as a keynote or conference speaker?
    Yes. For speaking engagements, please send a formal invitation to ipasha2001@uaf.edu.pk with event details including dates, venue, topic, and expected audience. Use "Speaking / Conference Invitation" in the contact form above.
