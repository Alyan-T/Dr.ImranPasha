Research Focus

Domains of Expertise

Prof. Pasha's work spans the breadth of food and nutrition science, with particular depth in grain quality, food safety, and analytical method development.

Grain Science

Cereal quality & characterization, wheat quality, milling and baking science, value addition of cereal brans and by-products.

Food Safety & Security

Pesticide residues, heavy metals, food fortification, composite flour technology, and malnutrition mitigation strategies.

Analytical Techniques

Chromatography (GC-MS, HPLC), spectroscopy (FTIR, atomic absorption & emission), and electrophoresis (SDS-PAGE).

Functional Foods & Nutraceuticals

Development of bioactive-enriched products, oleoresin characterization, and plant-based food innovation.

Human Nutrition

Dietary pattern assessment, school-age nutrition programs, malnutrition prevention, and micronutrient fortification strategies.

Food Technology & Processing

Hurdle technology, preservation techniques, bioactive films, composite flour development, and sustainable food processing.
